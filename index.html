Built for the realities of ground operations
Community-led searches still rely on fragmented updates, scattered notes and disconnected tools.

GRIDSEARCH is being developed to unify the core elements of a live operation — people, search assignments, locations and updates — into a single operational picture. When time is critical, coordination must be instantly actionable and structured to support high-stakes decisions.

Web  ·  iOS  ·  Android
  • 01
    One shared operational view
    One shared operational view enables coordinators to deploy volunteers more effectively and make best use of available resources to maximise ground coverage. Without high-reliability positioning and verified sector tracking, critical areas are missed and ground is duplicated.
  • 02
    Volunteer safety net
    Built to support volunteers in all environments, with safety treated as a non-negotiable part of the coordination model rather than an afterthought.
  • 03
    Court-Admissible Logs
    GRIDSEARCH maintains a full chronological record of events in a tamper-evident format, securely stored, enabling activity to be reviewed, compared, and exported if required for legal scrutiny or investigation.
Operational Architecture

One platform.
Built for the field.

GRIDSEARCH is designed around how community searches actually happen: rapidly, under pressure, across mixed teams, where coordination must be intuitive and immediate.

⚡
Fast Incident Setup
Mobilise an operation in seconds without specialist training or complex configuration.
⊞
Live Operational Overview
Bring search activity, assignments, and updates into one coordinated view for the Initiator.
📶
Offline-First Burst Sync
Ensures operational continuity in signal dead zones. Architecture is designed to inherit Satellite D2C connectivity transparently as it becomes available.
🎙
Voice-to-Text Reporting
Hands-free field updates with AI auto-categorisation for sightings, clues, and hazards.
🛡
Volunteer Safety Net
Proactive monitoring with inactivity alerts and automated fall detection.
⚖
Court-Admissible Audit Trail
Every action is recorded in an immutable, tamper-evident log for legal and insurance review.
Probabilistic Modelling

Future-Ready Behavioural Intelligence.

GRIDSEARCH is designed to evolve into smarter search coordination over time, including structured behavioural insight, clearer prioritisation, and stronger operational learning.

🔍
Human search behaviour models
Structured human subject profiles to support smarter search prioritisation and decision-making.
🐕
Animal search intelligence
Specialist animal behaviour profiles for canine, wildlife and livestock search operations.
🦅
Smarter search prioritisation
Probability-based sector prioritisation to help coordinators direct resources to the highest-value areas first.
🏘
Better post-incident learning
Structured post-incident data to support review, improvement and stronger operational records over time.
