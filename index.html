The Coordination Gap
Moving beyond fragmented informal tools to a unified, structured coordination engine that facilitates complex searches with ease.

Most community-led searches are coordinated without purpose-built tools. GRIDSEARCH is the structured alternative — a coordination engine that provides a holistic view of the operation, simplifying the decision process during highly stressful, time-critical incidents. What GRIDSEARCH Does

One platform.
Built for the field.

A complete coordination engine for community-led search — operable from any device, in any terrain, with no heavy training required. A Decision Support System that provides a holistic view of the operation, simplifying the decision process during highly stressful, time-critical incidents. Coordinators retain full operational authority at all times.

⊞
Dynamic Coverage Tracking
Real-time sector tracking using High-Reliability Global Positioning — utilising redundant location data to ensure maximum accuracy in challenging terrain. Burst Sync architecture ensures data integrity whenever network or satellite connectivity is available.
◉
Volunteer Safety Monitoring
Fall detection, inactivity alerts, and an SOS emergency button. The Hard-Cut Protocol is an architectural enforcement: GPS tracking is programmatically severed the moment a volunteer presses End Search — not a configurable setting. Total volunteer privacy, by design.
⚖
Court-Admissible Audit Trail
Every action logged with an immutable, hash-verified timestamp from day one. UTC-standardised for cross-border legal consistency. Structured for legal proceedings, inquests, and agency handover.
📋
One-Tap Field Reporting
Sightings, clues, hazards, and assistance requests captured in a single tap — with integrated photo capture and voice-to-text for hands-free reporting. GPS-tagged and timestamped automatically. Utilises an automated client-side compression protocol to ensure the platform remains light and performant during high-volume incidents.
📡
Offline-First & Satellite-Ready
Purpose-built for environments without reliable signal. Data is stored locally and synchronised automatically whenever connectivity returns. Satellite direct-to-cell compatible by design — no rebuild required.
⚙
Optimised Field Interface
Glove-friendly 64×64 dp touch targets designed for rapid onboarding with no heavy training required. Night and day visual optimisation. Reliable regardless of weather conditions or environment.
◈
Probabilistic Search Overlays
A Decision Support System providing coordinators with a holistic view and data-driven prioritisation.
